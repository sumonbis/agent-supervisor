## Trust, but verify

Each scenario plays out an agent working on a real task. At key moments it pauses
and asks **you** to decide:

- Do you **trust** the agent's claim, or **verify** it?
- Do you **allow**, **warn**, or **block** a risky action?
- Do you **approve** the plan, or send it back?

After every choice you'll see what *actually* happened — and your **trust calibration**
score updates. The goal isn't to distrust everything; it's to trust the right amount,
at the right moment.
